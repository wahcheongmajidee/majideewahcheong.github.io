About Us

Wah Cheong Bicycle Shop is committed to providing customers with the best products and services. Mountain bikes, road bikes and various accessories can be found in Wah Cheong.

In the early days, the Chinese had just arrived in Malaysia. The founder of Wah Cheong Bicycle Shop hoped that the Chinese would prosper in Malaysia, so he name it Wah Cheong. Wah represents Chinese and Cheong represents prosperity.

Our store will provide good repair services for all bicycles sold by this store, and there will be no charge for bicycles purchased in this store if there is no need to replace parts.
